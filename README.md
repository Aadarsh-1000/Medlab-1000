# MEDLAB
MEDLAB is a medical diagnosis engine that uses various .json files measuring up to 10 million lines borrowed from open-source DOID.


Hey there, I am Aadarsh, the creator and owner of MEDLAB. My goal is to make this a reliable and stable diagnosis engine in the near future. The reason that I wanted to build a diagnosis engine is because I am interested in web design and medicine. I also needed to get some exposure to JS. Anyways, the project turned out a lot better than expected.

## HOW I GOT THE IDEA
I got the idea of MEDLAB when I was thinking of ways to get a free diagnosis, so I started brainstorming with my relatives (med students). Thanks to them, I got the idea to make MEDLAB.
